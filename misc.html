Miscellaneous

Here are some ideas and concepts from various courses I've taken at Texas State University.

Cigarette Packaging

Packaging concept done for Holy Smokes. The idea is a play on the word 'Holy' and is meant to look like a black leather bible with gold lettering. A little morbid I know, but I think it fits with the theme of cigarettes and the affects they are known to cause.

Glass Straws Advertisement

Hummingbird is a manufacturer of reusable, glass straws in an aim to reduce the impact of plastic pollution. This poster was created as the start of an ad campain for them.
